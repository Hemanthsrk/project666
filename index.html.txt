public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="6tlr1-0-0"><span data-offset-key="6tlr1-0-0">• To make it easy for users to find you so they can contact you (i.e., an online PR tool)</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="b3bvh-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="b3bvh-0-0"><span data-offset-key="b3bvh-0-0">These are all important functions of websites; however, it also has many other functions such as: • Providing a place where people can find information about all things related to the business (i.e., an e-Commerce site) • Explaining how products work inside the computer system (which is important when selling those computers) • Providing an idea of how people will use the product once they get it (which is important when selling those computers) • Ensuring that users are able to access this site properly once they arrive at it so if anything goes wrong with any part of the site they will be able to find out what went wrong with their connection (which is important when selling those computers) • Ensuring that users are able to navigate between different parts of their website so if anything goes wrong with any part of the website they can go back up without losing their progress (which is important when selling those computers) • Providing some sort of user-friendly interface so that users don’t have too much trouble using the site when they first get it (which is important when selling those computers) • Ensuring that every aspect of this web site works in order for websites like yours to function properly which makes sure no user is having trouble accessing certain parts of your web site which in turn results in less traffic being funneled towards this web site which</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="58o91-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="58o91-0-0"><span data-offset-key="58o91-0-0">– Graphics</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="rsb6-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="rsb6-0-0"><span data-offset-key="rsb6-0-0">A website is essentially a webpage designed to be useful to those who visit it. Some websites, like those of a personal blog or an online store, do not require much in the way of content. However, there is a line between being useful and being useless. You can have the former with an informative page, but you can’t have the latter without being able to provide value for your visitors.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="2rfhq-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="2rfhq-0-0"><span data-offset-key="2rfhq-0-0">To find out what makes your website useful and how you can create it that way, we need to take a closer look at web design.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="8k26b-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="8k26b-0-0"><span data-offset-key="8k26b-0-0">The first steps in web design are selecting a font family and typeface that best represents your brand; choosing the right color palette for your site; and deciding what information needs to be included on the page in order to make it easy for readers to access.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="4oet5-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="4oet5-0-0"><span data-offset-key="4oet5-0-0">So think about what makes your business unique first — like what its products or services do that others don’t offer? Then, choose a color palette that will create visual interest based on this unique feature so you can position yourself as a leader in this category. Once you’ve placed yourself on the map of such industries as healthcare, mobile technology and real estate, why not use bright yet subtle colors when designing your site? Or perhaps something more complicated like white or dark blue?</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="s0kr-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="s0kr-0-0"><span data-offset-key="s0kr-0-0">And then there are elements that are simply components of what makes your business so special — things like custom headers, footers and CSS stylesheets — which allow you to customize how visitors navigate through your site. Most people won’t notice them unless they’re carefully set up in web browsers.</span></div></div></div></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-QdrKKLbrxdKNhRCR0dpGU" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-QdrKKLbrxdKNhRCR0dpGU" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-header"><h2 class="sppb-addon-title">Why you should care about web design</h2></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-1655459635672" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-1655459635672" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-text-block "><div class="sppb-addon-content"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="2dahb-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="2dahb-0-0"><span data-offset-key="2dahb-0-0">Web design is the process of creating a web site that allows the user to view, access and use it. Web Designers must create a website that reflects the organization’s identity, and uses content that is relevant to the type of business and how it can be used. The customer needs to be able to get information they want from their website, while also being able to navigate through it.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="af7t1-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="af7t1-0-0"><span data-offset-key="af7t1-0-0">Web Designers must have an understanding of physics, and how light effects the color of objects. They need to understand how people interact with websites, so their designs are more ergonomic. When designing websites for businesses, web designers need to be aware of specific target audiences for each site. A good web designer will know what kind of audience is most likely to purchase their products or services. This knowledge is important when designing a website for a specific type of business.</span></div></div></div></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-1655459635690" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-1655459635690" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-header"><h2 class="sppb-addon-title">The benefits of good web design</h2></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-1655459635699" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-1655459635699" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-text-block "><div class="sppb-addon-content"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><span data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0">Web design is one of the most important aspects of any company’s website. At the same time, it is also one of the least understood and most misunderstood.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="c5kch-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="c5kch-0-0"><span data-offset-key="c5kch-0-0">The reason why it is so difficult to master is because there are a lot of people who want to do it. We want to do it so badly that we start learning how to code, must learn how to design, and even how to build websites with our hands.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="nsdk-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="nsdk-0-0"><span data-offset-key="nsdk-0-0">But web design isn’t just an art form. It is a science as well.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="4hn8l-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="4hn8l-0-0"><span data-offset-key="4hn8l-0-0">As you will see below, there are some things that every web designer should know about web design and other non-web developers should learn before they can fully understand what makes for good web designs.</span></div></div></div></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-oqCXkcHrz2av30vO_sChM" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-oqCXkcHrz2av30vO_sChM" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-header"><h2 class="sppb-addon-title">The importance of mobile-friendly web design</h2></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-iiGPD0ZEA3n5mifAhcSmo" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-iiGPD0ZEA3n5mifAhcSmo" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-text-block "><div class="sppb-addon-content"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="7dt7m-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="7dt7m-0-0"><span data-offset-key="7dt7m-0-0">Before we dive into web design, we need to address a few things that don’t get talked about very often.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="3h573-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="3h573-0-0"><span data-offset-key="3h573-0-0">Mobile-friendly website design is nothing but a matter of personal preference. On one hand, you want your site to be easy to read. On the other hand, you don’t want to have people stop on your site and stare at an overwhelming amount of text.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="2jmmk-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="2jmmk-0-0"><span data-offset-key="2jmmk-0-0">Since there isn’t really a standard for mobile-friendly web design, we tend to use “viewport” as the tipping point when it comes to determining whether or not a site will be viewed as “mobile-friendly.” While viewport is indeed a critical aspect in determining the readability and accessibility of your site, it isn’t sufficient by itself — there are many other factors at play here including fonts and headers that do not visually translate well across all devices (e.g., emojis).</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="3dh98-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="3dh98-0-0"><span data-offset-key="3dh98-0-0">There is no right or wrong here, but it can be helpful if you understand some of the technical details behind everything involved — what fonts are used; whether or not you need to refer back to CSS in order to ensure that your website has been properly optimized for all browsers; what font sizes do you use; what elements do you keep from using if they aren’t supported by all browsers; etc.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="fr5b7-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="fr5b7-0-0"><span data-offset-key="fr5b7-0-0">The fact that most people don’t know much about web design doesn’t mean you should ignore them entirely. Much like love at first sight, something is bound to happen when two people meet each other for the first time. You may feel inspired by someone else; think about ideas together as a team; and just because something seems weird doesn’t mean it should stay weird forever (your mind works on this same principle). It does help if there is some mutual understanding between yourself and them on matters related to web design: simply talking with each other sometimes helps avoid accidental clichéd phrases being repeated ad nauseam until they start feeling redundant (and having these words repeated in front of them can give wings).</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="c9f62-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="c9f62-0-0"><span data-offset-key="c9f62-0-0">Keep in mind that while two people may be sharing their thoughts on an idea they had together, they are still two separate individuals who have made up their minds independently on how best they wish to express themselves — these remember that sometimes typing out words will just not come</span></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-tOaDQOSUOVEDlX0i6YlG6" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div class="sppb-addon-content"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="1a1dk-0-0"><span data-offset-key="1a1dk-0-0">The present web design landscape is a mishmash of many different types of websites. Each type of site has a different purpose and target audience. In this section, I’ll describe each type of website, how they work and what makes them special.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="72upr-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="72upr-0-0"><span data-offset-key="72upr-0-0">A website is typically divided into three areas:</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="be5h1-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="be5h1-0-0"><span data-offset-key="be5h1-0-0">-Home Page – Home Page is the main page or navigational area on your site. It lists all your products and services. The content for this area comes from the Content Management System (CMS).</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="bf7gq-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="bf7gq-0-0"><span data-offset-key="bf7gq-0-0">-About – This area lists the company’s background, history, achievements and other information. The content for this area comes from the Content Management System (CMS).</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="e56c-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="e56c-0-0"><span data-offset-key="e56c-0-0">-Products – This area lists all products that are offered by the company and their prices; made available to buyers through the CMS system or directly from the company website’s sales department. The content for this area comes from the Content Management System (CMS).</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="1fdb9-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="1fdb9-0-0"><span data-offset-key="1fdb9-0-0">So what are these different areas? The following is a breakdown of each page/section:</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="e6bel-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="e6bel-0-0"><span data-offset-key="e6bel-0-0">Home Page Home page as it appears on a typical website A home page consists mainly of text with some images and links to other pages within your website. The URL for your home page will start with www . For example: www .santaolive .com /home_page This home page usually contains an image that describes your product or service along with some information about you (your company) and how you can contact you if needed.</span></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-hBIADP-m-jzOdDff3Bst5" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-hBIADP-m-jzOdDff3Bst5" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-header"><h2 class="sppb-addon-title">Conclusion: why web design is essential</h2></div></div></div><div id="sppb-addon-wrapper-6-v7MvLzgI83X3_GzHXBl" class="sppb-addon-wrapper "><div id="sppb-addon-6-v7MvLzgI83X3_GzHXBl" class="clearfix  "><div class="sppb-addon sppb-addon-text-block "><div class="sppb-addon-content"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="f8rfb-0-0"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="7dt7m-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="7dt7m-0-0"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="1a1dk-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="1a1dk-0-0"><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="6jr7l-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="6jr7l-0-0"><span data-offset-key="6jr7l-0-0">This is a short article, but it is not just about web design. This is especially important for web designers — there aren’t too many of us and we don’t get enough attention from other people, which means we don’t get enough attention from ourselves.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="11msp-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="11msp-0-0"><span data-offset-key="11msp-0-0">I also want to know if you really need me in your life. This essay will be more about what businesses do not understand when looking at digital design — that it does not need a designer; that it can be done without any “hands on” involvement; that there are plenty of software packages out there that can do most of the work for you, without having to pay anyone else anything (and, yes, they do exist). A wide range of software packages allows businesses to create their own websites with minimal effort and very little money spent — or even no money spent at all if you use an “offline” CMS like WordPress or Joomla (which can also be used online). If you don’t believe me read this article on how e-commerce websites are being built by less than creative individuals using simple software tools.</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="6l0rl-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="6l0rl-0-0"><span data-offset-key="6l0rl-0-0">If you really want to know what’s wrong with business looking at website designs as something out of a book or magazine then read this article , which was written by someone who was once in charge of it (and who just happens to be an award winning author). At some point in time I might write another article on business looking at website designs as something out of a book or magazine, but right now let’s stay focused on these three things…</span></div></div><div class="" data-block="true" data-editor="4poh0" data-offset-key="46h4u-0-0"><div class="public-DraftStyleDefault-block public-DraftStyleDefault-ltr" data-offset-key="46h4u-0-0"><span data-offset-key="46h4u-0-0">1) website design should be free; 2) all businesses should pay for their websites; 3) there should definitely be some hands-on involvement. If any one of these three ideas doesn’t resonate with you then maybe your business doesn’t need a website designer after all…</span></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></section></div></div>                    </div>
